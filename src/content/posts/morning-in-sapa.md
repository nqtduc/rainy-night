The fog came up the valley before the sun did. I stood at the edge of the rice
terraces with a cup of tea going cold in my hands, watching the green steps
disappear and reappear.

There is a particular silence in the mountains here — not empty, but full of
small sounds. A rooster somewhere below. Water moving through the channels cut
into the hillside generations ago.

> I didn't take many photos that morning. Some mornings are for keeping, not
> capturing.

By the time the mist lifted, the whole valley was awake. I walked down slowly,
in no hurry to be anywhere.
